About

I’m a developer focused on building thoughtful mobile apps and game systems, with most of my work centered around Flutter and Unity. I like making software that feels clear, polished, and deliberate. I care about structure, usability, and the small details that make an interface feel calm to use. Whether I’m working on an app or a game, I usually aim for simple architecture, tidy interactions, and steady iteration. My stack includes Flutter, Dart, Unity, C#, and the tools needed to keep projects practical and maintainable. Outside of code, I’m usually drawn to projects with atmosphere, systems, and a quiet sense of purpose.
